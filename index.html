TeamWard features

There are plenty of tools to get information on your current game, but most of them are too easy to forget.
TeamWard will send you a push notification, directly to your smartphone, as soon as you enter the loading screen. No one likes being "first blooded", and TeamWard will give you the information you need to stay safe and win the game.
TeamWard will also help you learn champions you're unfamiliar with: do you need magic resist or armor? Is this champion good for splipushing, poking, disengaging?
Finally, you'll also get tips on your team and the ennemy team -- no jungler? Full AP team? Premade 5? Everything you need, right at your fingertip.
